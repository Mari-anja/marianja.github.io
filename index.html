ABOUT

ABOUT

ABOUT

Im the founder of Riwora, an AI-driven platform designed to take the hassle out of follow-ups and repetitive tasks, so salespeople can focus on what truly matters, building meaningful client relationships and closing deals.

My journey is a blend of design and creating. I started in Georgia, designing spaces and products, and eventually found myself in the world of sales here in the U.S. Along the way, I realized how much potential there was to simplify and improve the way sales teams work. Thats where Riwora came in, a platform inspired by real challenges I experienced and built with the goal of making a meaningful difference for businesses.
